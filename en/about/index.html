Hello! I’m an English major. My hobbies include listening to music, taking walks, watching movies, and reading novels. I especially enjoy English-language songs, and my favorite films are the seven “Harry Potter” movies.

Lately I’ve taken a liking to writing, and I hope to improve my writing skills by blogging.

This site uses the Hugo PaperMod theme. Previously I used the Hexo Butterfly theme, but it wasn’t minimalist enough, so I switched to PaperMod. The site has two separate pages—one in Chinese and one in English—which are independent of each other rather than direct translations.

On the Chinese page, I share book notes, travel experiences, reflections, and other aspects of daily life. On the English page, I write short stories to practice my grammar and vocabulary.

I also hope to meet like-minded people through this site. You can see the tools and software I commonly use in this article.
