The Witches

June 10, 2025 · 1 min · 106 words

Recently I read The Witches, an insteresting story. The story is about a boy whose parents died in an accident, and he stayed with his grandmother. While they were on vacation, the boy was turned into a mouse by the witches using a potion called Mouse-Maker. Then he and his grandmother made a plan to put Mouse-Maker into the witches’ dinner. Then they succeeded and wanted to get rid of all the witches in the world.

For me, the reading level of this book is appropriate, so I could read it fluently. If you want to improve English level, reading English novels is a great way.
